    Content Strategy Forum Cape Town October 2012

    In October 2012, the international content strategy conference will be packing its bags and heading south, for a glorious summer holiday in one of the most beautiful cities in the world: Cape Town, South Africa.watch videos and listen to recordings of the presentations on the schedule page.

    Here's what we can tell you so far. There will be:

    • presentations and workshops by international authorities on content strategy, UX, design, and more
    • panoramic mash-ups of mountains, blue skies, winelands and the Atlantic Ocean
    • music and merriment under the African night-sky
    • braai chops, boerewors and biltong, washed down with plenty of excellent Cape wine
    • the chance to hang out with both local and international content enthusiasts
    • the option of organised sightseeing activities before and after the conference
    • a uniquely South African flavour to the whole experience
